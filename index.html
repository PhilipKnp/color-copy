ColorCopy.io

with ColorCopy.io you can get any Color Code from Screenshots. Take a Screenshot of your color with the instructions below and click on it. You will then see a hex, rgba and hsla value which you can copy and use in your projects.

- 1. Take a Screenshot of the Color you want -

Windows:  ⊞ Win+⇧ Shift+S

Mac OS:  ⌘+⇧ Shift+4

Linux:  Alt+PrintScr

- 2. Then Paste it -

Windows & Linux:  Ctrl+V

Mac OS:  ⌘+V
